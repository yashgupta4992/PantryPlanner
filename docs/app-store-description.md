## Full Description (Up to 4000 characters)

### Headline
Your personal kitchen operating system that plans meals, organizes shopping, and reduces food waste.

### Main Description

**PantryPlanner** is the all-in-one kitchen companion for families who want to:
- Plan weekly meals effortlessly
- Shop smarter with automatically organized lists
- Reduce food waste with expiry tracking
- Cook from what you have at home

**The Problem We Solve**

Every week, families waste money and food while asking the same questions:
- "What should we cook this week?"
- "What groceries do we need?"
- "What's about to expire?"

PantryPlanner answers all three with one beautifully designed app.

**What You Can Do**

📖 **Recipe Library**
Store all your family's favorite recipes in one place. Add photos, ingredients, cooking time, and dietary tags. Mark your go-to meals and track how often you cook them.

📅 **Weekly Meal Planner**
Plan your week visually. Drag and drop meals into a Mon–Sun calendar. Adjust serving sizes, swap meals, or copy last week's plan to save time. See your entire week at a glance.

🛒 **Smart Grocery Lists**
Get automatically generated shopping lists from your meal plan. The app intelligently consolidates duplicate ingredients across meals, groups items by category, and lets you check them off while shopping.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Family Profiles**
Set dietary preferences, allergies, and cuisine favorites for your household. Share your meal plan and grocery lists with family members across their devices.

⏰ **Expiry Tracking** (Coming in Phase 2)
Log what you buy and when. Get alerts for items expiring soon. Discover recipes that use your at-risk ingredients to minimize waste.

🤖 **AI-Powered Suggestions** (Coming in Phase 2)
Get personalized meal recommendations based on your cooking history, dietary preferences, and what you have in stock. Powered by advanced AI.
