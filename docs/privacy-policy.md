# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to PantryPlanner ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Name and email address (if you create an account)
- Family profile information (household size, dietary preferences, cuisine preferences)

**Content You Create:**
- Recipes (name, ingredients, instructions, photos)
- Meal plans and weekly schedules
- Grocery lists
- Pantry inventory items with expiry dates
- Cooking history and preferences

**Device Information:**
- Device type, operating system, and version
- Device identifiers (if permitted by your privacy settings)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Features you access and how you interact with them
- Time and duration of app usage
- Actions taken within the app (creating recipes, planning meals, etc.)

**Technical Information:**
- IP address (for CloudKit sync)
- Crash reports and performance data
- Analytics related to app functionality

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery**: To provide, maintain, and improve the PantryPlanner app
- **Personalization**: To tailor content and features to your preferences
- **Family Sharing**: To enable CloudKit sync across your family's devices
- **AI Features**: To power meal suggestions and recipe recommendations (Phase 2+)
- **Analytics**: To understand app usage and improve user experience
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Security**: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### On-Device Storage
- Your recipes, meal plans, and pantry data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- This data is encrypted by iOS's standard encryption mechanisms

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you enable family sharing, your data is synced via CloudKit to Apple's servers
- CloudKit uses end-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Data is retained as long as your account is active

### Data Deletion
- You can delete individual items within the app at any time
- Deleting the app will remove locally stored data from your device
- To fully delete your account and cloud data, contact us (see Section 10)

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Share Your Information:
- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not rent or lease your information

### We MAY Share Information:

**With Family Members:**
- Only if you enable family sharing features
- Shared data is limited to what you explicitly share (recipes, meal plans, grocery lists)

**With Service Providers:**
- Apple (CloudKit storage and sync)
- Anthropic (Claude API) - only for AI-powered meal suggestions, and only with your explicit consent
- Analytics providers (usage patterns, non-personal)

**Legal Requirements:**
- If required by law, court order, or government request
- To protect our legal rights and the safety of our users

## 6. Third-Party Services

### Apple CloudKit
- Used for optional family sync and multi-device support
- Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

### Anthropic Claude API (Phase 2+)
- Used for AI meal suggestions and recommendations
- Data sent to Claude API is limited to your dietary preferences and cooking history
- Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

### Analytics Services
- We may use aggregated, non-personal analytics to understand app usage
- No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Local Encryption**: SwiftData uses iOS's native encryption
- **Transport Security**: TLS 1.2+ for all data transmission
- **CloudKit Encryption**: Apple's end-to-end encryption for synced data
- **Access Controls**: Only you and family members (if enabled) can access your data

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
