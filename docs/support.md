## 1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: Is PantryPlanner free?**
A: Yes! PantryPlanner offers a free tier with essential features. A premium subscription is available for advanced features like AI meal suggestions and family sharing.

**Q: What devices does PantryPlanner support?**
A: PantryPlanner is currently available for iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) running iOS 14 or later.

**Q: Can I use PantryPlanner offline?**
A: Yes! All your recipes, meal plans, and pantry data are stored locally on your device. You can use the app without an internet connection. Family sharing requires internet for CloudKit sync.

### Account & Data

**Q: How do I enable family sharing?**
A: Go to Settings → Family Sharing and invite family members. They'll need to accept the invitation on their devices. All data will sync via CloudKit.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact us at support@pantryplanner.app or use the "Delete Account" option in Settings. This will remove all your data from our servers.

**Q: Can I export my recipes and data?**
A: You can share individual recipes via AirDrop or email. Full data export is available in the Premium tier.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Why isn't my data syncing across devices?**
A: 
- Ensure both devices are signed into the same iCloud account
- Check that you have an active internet connection
- Enable CloudKit sync in Settings
- Try signing out and back into your iCloud account

**Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?**
A: 
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Try restarting your device
3. Make sure your iOS is up to date
4. If the issue persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Please describe the issue in detail and contact us at support@pantryplanner.app or open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## 7. Troubleshooting Guide

### App Won't Open
- Force close the app: Swipe up from the bottom of your screen (or use app switcher) and swipe up on PantryPlanner
- Restart your device
- Update to the latest version of PantryPlanner from the App Store
- Uninstall and reinstall the app (this will not delete your data if synced to CloudKit)

### Can't Create a Recipe
- Ensure you have sufficient storage on your device
- Try restarting the app
- Check that your device has at least 50MB of free storage

### Meal Plan Not Showing
- Refresh the app by closing and reopening it
- Check that you've selected recipes for the days
- Ensure your device date and time are correct

### Family Member Can't See Shared Data
- Verify they're invited to family sharing
- Ask them to restart their device
- Check that they're signed into the correct iCloud account
- Wait up to 5 minutes for CloudKit to sync

### Photos Not Appearing
- Ensure the app has permission to access photos (Settings → PantryPlanner → Photos)
- Try taking a new photo instead of using an existing one
- Check that the image file is not corrupted

## 8. Performance Tips

To get the best experience with PantryPlanner:

1. **Keep iOS Updated:** Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version
2. **Free Up Space:** Maintain at least 100MB of free storage
3. **Restart Regularly:** Restart your device periodically
4. **Clear Cache:** Go to Settings → PantryPlanner → Clear Cache
5. **Optimize Photos:** Use smaller image files for recipes
6. **Check Network:** Use a stable WiFi connection for family sync
